# HR Performance Review Format — 2026

## Manager Review — Open Questions

### 1. Job Mastery

**Rate the individual's Job Mastery, based on the metrics below:**

- Demonstrates deep understanding of their domain, applying relevant methods, tools, and best practices to produce excellent work
- Makes thoughtful, transparent trade-offs (e.g. simplicity vs. complexity) and evaluates the rationale behind decisions
- Identifies opportunities to connect to other tasks — such as accuracy, reliability, maintainability, security, accessibility, or data integrity
- Clearly communicates reasoning and decisions through appropriate formats (e.g. memos, specs, data, code, research, testing, or metrics) to support decisions and educate outcomes
- Questions assumptions and suggests better or smarter ways to work to add/multiply
- Learns from feedback and experiences to improve the quality, efficiency, and impact of their work

**Rating:** Not Meeting Expectations / Partially Meeting Expectations / Meeting Expectations / Exceeding Expectations / Legendary

**Comment:** *(required if Not Meeting, Partially Meeting, Exceeding, or Legendary)*

---

### 2. Execution and Outcomes

**Rate the individual's Execution and Outcomes, based on the metrics below:**

- Hitting committed objectives, controls scope and ensures on-time delivery
- Planning proactively and adapting to unplanned situations and shifts
- Owning blockers and driving resolutions proactively
- Clear on progress, completion, throughput, and accountability for results
- Measurable customer or business impact tied to roadmap or OKRs

**Rating:** Not Meeting Expectations / Partially Meeting Expectations / Meeting Expectations / Exceeding Expectations / Legendary

**Comment:** *(required if Not Meeting, Partially Meeting, Exceeding, or Legendary)*

---

### 3. People, Collaboration and Influence Skills

**Rate the individual's People, Collaboration and Influence Skills based on the metrics below:**

- Active listening and communication
- Constructive handling of feedback, disagreement, and conflict
- Building and maintaining collaborative and respectful relationships based on reliability, inclusivity, and inclusion
- Cross-team collaboration and stakeholder alignment to achieve shared goals
- Mentoring, coaching, and living responsibility to strengthen others' capabilities
- Leading by example and influencing others without relying on authority

**Rating:** Not Meeting Expectations / Partially Meeting Expectations / Meeting Expectations / Exceeding Expectations / Legendary

**Comment:** *(required if Not Meeting, Partially Meeting, Exceeding, or Legendary)*

---

## Culture and Value Demonstration

*Please provide the overall comments below.*

### 1. Does the individual demonstrate the value "Own the Outcome"?

**Rating:** Not Demonstrated / Partially Demonstrated / Demonstrated / Exemplary

### 2. Does the individual demonstrate the value "Start with the Customer"?

**Rating:** Not Demonstrated / Partially Demonstrated / Demonstrated / Exemplary

### 3. Does the individual demonstrate the value "Speak Up, Team Up"?

**Rating:** Not Demonstrated / Partially Demonstrated / Demonstrated / Exemplary

### 4. Does the individual demonstrate the value "Think Deep, Move Fast"?

**Rating:** Not Demonstrated / Partially Demonstrated / Demonstrated / Exemplary

### 5. Does the individual demonstrate the value "Anything is Possible"?

**Rating:** Not Demonstrated / Partially Demonstrated / Demonstrated / Exemplary

### 6. Evaluating Values — Overall Comment

Provide your overall ratings across the five company values. While we encourage managers that their values ratings together, please include your observations and differentiation so to help it stand out.

**Tips:**
- If "Exemplary" — add why it's out of the norm
- If they show leadership in ownership, influencing cross-team standards — highlight it
- If "Not Demonstrated" — avoid judgment, state behaviors (e.g., "Rarely participated in team discussions") and flag early (no need to assess punishments or repercussions)

---

## Growth & Development

### 7. Growth & Development

- **Growth Focus:** Identify 1–2 specific areas (skills, behaviors, or mindsets) to develop next.
- **Action or Experience:** Describe how they might grow that area (practice, project, coaching, etc.)
- **Why it Matters:** Link to their next-level goals or team impact.
